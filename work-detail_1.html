Exploring Digital Newsroom

Texas Department of Transportation



How to

By client kickoff meeting I learned that Texas Department of Transportation (TxDOT) wants to deliver the messages on its own will, instead of being controlled by local media. Also, they want to raise its brand awareness and bridge the communication gap between TxDOT and the public.


Problem Space:
TxDOT is having trouble relying on the local media to spread the brand message because the local media is not motivated to promote the positive spin TxDOT wants to apply. However, having no idea of what would take to open their own Digital/Corporate Newsroom.

How I do:
After sorting out several aspects of research questions using affinity diagram, I strategized several research methods to focus on different problems, including both generative and evaluative research. I performed early research about the elements of a good online newsroom to clarify the research problem. Then, I used subject matter expert to gain insights and conducted interview and surveys for average drivers to know how they plan for a trip and find out more about driver’s behaviors. I also executed user testing to figure out the pain points of the current website.

My Role:
Research, Subject matter expert (SME) interviews, Competitive Analysis, Survey, 1-1 interviews, Usability test, Quantitative data analysis

Tools:
Sketch, Qualtrics, Zoom, UserZoom

What I learn:
What I learned from this project is how to use different methods to solve problems. Also, using Sketch to compose a complete final report.

I combined both generative and formative research to solve the exisitng issues.



By Affinity Diagram to grasp a higher level of questions and narrow down our problem scope



From Subject Matter Expert, I would like to know what consists a digital newsroom and what is the relationship between different platforms. I interviewed tow experts who both have professional experience on newsroom and collected information of what are the elements of a great digital newsroom should have and gain insights of what question should be put on the survey and interview.



From Survey, I want to determine what are the contents that the users are interested in receiving it and device preference of searching information. I launched the survey using Qualtrics and asked questions about what kinds of information do users look up in daily life and what are the sources do they use.

Then, I want to ascertain how users perceive TxDOT and where do they find the information. I conducted 1-1 interview by asking participants what are their typical driving behaviors, how do they plan on the trip and what do they know about TxDOT


I performed Usability Testing on current TxDOT website to determine how the users are using TxDOT website and what do they feel. I asked participants to share their screens, opened up the TxDOT website and walked me through two tasks using Userzoom. After the tasks were complete, filled out the 5-point Likert scale and further elaborated “why”.
